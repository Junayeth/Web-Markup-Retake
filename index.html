Victron SmartSolar

MPPT 100-50 12V 24V Charge Controller

The Victron SmartSolar MPPT 100/50 charge control is equipped with the MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracker) system. When it's cloudy and the light intensity varies continuously, the MPPT control system (Maximum Power Point Tracker) enhances the energy yield. The technology ensures that the charge regulator automatically adjusts to those changing voltages so the energy is transferred efficiently from the solar panel to the battery. By making use of a charge controller with MPPT system, you can consume up to 30 percent more power from solar panels. The Bluetooth Smart charge controller has a built-in bluetooth module so the return (real time and history) of your solar panels is easy to read in the Victron program. The program is suitable for both Apple and Android smartphones, tablet computers or other apparatus.

Victron Solar panel set

175Wp 12Volt Panel

The Victron Energy BlueSolar 175Wp poly solar panel requires a very low light output because the solar panel has a high sensitivity across the entire spectrum. The solar panel has a size of 1485 x 668 x 30 millimeters. The closed, watertight connection box creates a high level of safety. The anodised aluminum frame makes it easy to mount modules on a roof. The hardened glass is of a very high quality and provides an improved impact resistance and rigidity of the panel. The BlueSolar solar panel has a nominal power of 175 Watt, an open terminal voltage of 23.7 Volt and a maximum power current of 9.03 Amps. The MC4 quick-connect system is pre-wired with PV-ST01 plugs.

Mounting is possible in various ways. The panel is equipped with 4 oval holes that can be used for mounting.

Victron Smart Inverter

12V 2000W 230V~

The Victron Phoenix 12/2000 Smart Inverter is a pure sine wave inverter that converts a 12 V battery voltage to a reliable 230 V 50/60 Hz voltage. In addition, the Phoenix 12/2000 provides 1600 Watts of continuous power (2000VA) and 4000 Watts of peak power. This allows you to connect and power 230V equipment via your 12V battery. The Phoenix 12/2000 Smart inverter is equipped with integrated Bluetooth and a VE.Direct communication port. This inverter with 2000VA (1600 Watt) of power is also perfectly suitable for appliances such as the Nespresso and Senseo coffee machines.

This inverter is fully configurable with a tablet or smartphone via Bluetooth.
